How EventForce Works

Companies post event jobs

SMEs or large companies post the available positions for their events, such as brand promoters, crew members and even waiters.

Jobseekers apply online

Interested jobseekers can apply easily through the regulated EventForce platform. Companies will automatically receive profiles and relevant information of applicants.

Job is matched!

Once companies decide who they want to hire, notifications will be sent to the applicants. After the job has been carried out, both companies and event worker can review each other.
